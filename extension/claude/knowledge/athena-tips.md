# Athena EMR Quick Reference

## Common Navigation

### Patient Chart
- **Chart Overview**: Summary of patient info, problems, meds, allergies
- **Clinical Desktop**: Main documentation area
- **Encounters**: Visit history and documentation

### Orders
- **Order Entry**: Use the "Orders" button or Alt+O
- **Lab Orders**: Orders > Lab > Search for test
- **Imaging Orders**: Orders > Imaging > Select modality

### Scheduling
- **Schedule Appointment**: Schedule > New Appointment
- **Surgery Scheduling**: Often requires OR coordinator

## Vascular-Specific Tips

### Ordering Studies
- **Arterial Duplex**: Orders > Imaging > US Arterial Lower Extremity
- **Venous Duplex**: Orders > Imaging > US Venous Lower Extremity
- **CTA**: Orders > Imaging > CTA (specify runoff vs aorta)
- **ABIs**: Orders > Cardiac/Vascular > ABI

### Common ICD-10 Codes
- I70.0 - Atherosclerosis of aorta
- I70.2 - Atherosclerosis of arteries of extremities
- I73.9 - Peripheral vascular disease
- I71.4 - Abdominal aortic aneurysm
- I65.2 - Carotid artery stenosis

### Pre-Op Documentation
1. H&P in Clinical Desktop
2. Consent form in Documents
3. Pre-op checklist (if required)
4. Anesthesia clearance note

## Keyboard Shortcuts
- Alt+O: Orders
- Alt+N: New Note
- Alt+S: Sign/Submit
- Ctrl+F: Find in chart

## Tips for Efficiency
- Use "Favorites" for common orders
- Create SmartPhrases for repetitive text
- Use patient lists for panel management
- Check "Sticky Notes" for nursing alerts
